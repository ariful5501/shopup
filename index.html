What is ShopUp?
ShopUp is Bangladesh's leading full-stack B2B commerce platform for small businesses. Our goal is to use technology to supercharge businesses with easy access to B2B sourcing and last-mile logistics.
Mokam - our B2B commerce platform serves small neighborhood shops, providing access to a wide range of products - all available at the tap of a button.
REDX - our logistics service - supercharges SMEs and large enterprises alike with the widest last-mile logistics network in Bangladesh.
Onkur - our financing platform aims to make relevant fintech solutions accessible for CMSMEs and agri-businesses.

ONKUR

Onkur helped me scale up

After starting my business, one of the biggest challenges I faced was scaling up sales so I could ultimately increase my profits. I realized the only way to scale up would be to invest more capital into the business so I could purchase more supplies, and in turn, make more sales. But no financial institution was ready to provide credit to a small trader like me. Onkur’s micro factoring product eliminated the need for more capital by simply providing me supplies on credit. This helped me significantly increase my sales and become a much more profitable business.

Anita Das Rani, Purba Store
